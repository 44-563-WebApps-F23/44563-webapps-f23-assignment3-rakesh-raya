My favourite vacation spot is Munnar located in Kerala

Munnar is a hill station which is called as God's own country that is situated at an altitude of 6000 feet. It is breathtakingly beautiful - a haven of peace and tranquility. It has vast tea plantations on the slopes of the hills making it eye feast. The aroma around the station is so pleasant which ties us more and more to that location. Munnar can not be described in words which can only be experienced. The hills, the mist, the valleys, the streams, the waterfalls, tea plantations are unforgettable scenaries forever in my mind.
