My favourite vacation spot is Munnar located in Kerala

Munnar is a hill station which is called as best destination in God's own country Kerala that is situated at an altitude of 6000 feet. It is breathtakingly beautiful - a haven of peace and tranquility. It has vast tea plantations on the slopes of the hills making it eye feast. The aroma around the town is so pleasant which ties us more and more to that location. Munnar can not be described in words which can only be experienced. The hills, the mist, the valleys, the streams, the waterfalls, tea plantations are unforgettable scenaries forever in my mind.

Things to do & Dishes in Munnar :

  1. Walking through the tea estates
  2. Enjoying the splashing water falls
  3. Trekking the hills
  4. Involving with the nature
  • Ari Pathri and Chicken Curry
  • Appam and prawn curry
  • Idiyappam with Egg Curry
  • NadanKozhi Varuthathu
  • Kerala Style Fish
