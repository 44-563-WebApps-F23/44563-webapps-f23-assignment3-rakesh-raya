Simon Ranger

Simon was born in a family which stayed Countryside close to the Forest. His father used to earn his livelihood from the sources available in the forest like Honey, Medicinal plants which does not involve deforestation as he had strong love for the nature. Derived from his father, Simon Ranger had a great love for the nature. He has a strong bond with the animals and prefered to stay in the forest whenever he has time during his childhood. So, he decided to find a profession that stays close to the forest and he made his job as Ranger who protects the national parks and natural areas.

Traits of Simon Ranger

  1. He is a Nature lover
  2. He is physically tall and strong
  3. He has great Empathy for animals
